# Feature Ideas

## Social / Sharing
- **Share recipes** — ability to share a cocktail recipe link with others (public or per-user)
- **Share a bar** — link multiple accounts in the same household to a shared bar inventory, while keeping individual ratings/bookmarks/favorites separate

## Bar Management
- **Image recognition for bar setup** — take a photo of your bar/shelf and auto-populate your virtual bar with detected bottles. High complexity; open questions include whether we need to maintain our own product image dataset or if an existing open-source set covers enough spirits/liqueurs.

## Planning
- **Tonight's cart** — a lightweight "queue" for recipes you're interested in making that night; easy to add/remove, separate from bookmarks/favorites
